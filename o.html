Product details

Oreo is a brand of sandwich cookie made up of two chocolate or golden cookie pieces with a sweet white creme filling in between. Nabisco first introduced Oreos in New York City in 1912 and they've been a favorite in the United States for over a century. As of December 2023, an estimated 500 billion Oreos have been sold, making them the world's best-selling cookie. Oreos come in many flavors and shapes, including chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, caramel coconut, chocolate marshmallow, blueberry, and green tea ice cream.
