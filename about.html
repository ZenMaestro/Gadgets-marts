BigBasket is an Indian online grocer headquartered in Bangalore, India, and currently owned by Tata Digital.[1] It was the first online grocer in India, set up in 2011. It is a registered company with the name Supermarket Grocery Supplies Pvt. Ltd.[10] As of January 2023, the company operates in more than 30 cities in India and processes around 15 million orders per month.[1] According to Bloomberg News, BigBasket is considering an initial public offering by 2025 as part of its growth plans.[3][11]
